Attractions

The Acharya Jagadish Chandra Bose Indian Botanic Garden offers a variety of attractions:

  • Beautiful Nature: The garden is home to a diverse range of flora and fauna.
  • Sunrise and Sunsets: Experience breathtaking views of the sunrise and sunset.
  • Bird Watching: The garden is a paradise for bird watchers, with a variety of bird species.
