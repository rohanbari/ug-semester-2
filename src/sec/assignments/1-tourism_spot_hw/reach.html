How to Reach from Kolkata

You can reach the Acharya Jagadish Chandra Bose Indian Botanic Garden by:

  • Car: A 45-minute drive from the city center.
  • Bus: Regular buses are available from various parts of Kolkata.
  • Train: The nearest railway station is Howrah, from where you can take a taxi or a bus.
